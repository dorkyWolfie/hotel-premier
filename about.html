About us

Hotel Premier is located in the heart of Skopje, the capital of Macedonia. 4 star hotel, which is very easy to reach from the A4 national road and is located 20km from the Airport Alexander the Great, walking distance from the main bus and trail station and from main city square. The marvelous ambience, atmosphere and high quality service makes Hotel Premier an excellent choice for Your trip.

Hotel Premier invites You to enjoy a warm welcome by the hotel staff and relax in one of the 13 different types of rooms equipped with an elegant décor with modern furnishings , private bathroom, conditioning, cable TV, mini bar , direct telephone line, safe box and Wi-Fi Internet connection.
